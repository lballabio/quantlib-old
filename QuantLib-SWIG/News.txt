
Release 1.6 - June 2015

- in sync with QuantLib 1.6;
- upgraded to SWIG 3.0.5;
- exported Gaussian1DModel, GSR, GsrProcess and
  Gaussian1dSwaptionEngine (thanks to Matthias Groncki);

- exported new IDR, MYR, RUB and VND currencies;
