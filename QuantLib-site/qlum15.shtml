QuantLib User Meeting 2015

The QuantLib User Meeting 2015 will be held in Düsseldorf on November 30th and December 1st, thanks to the sponsorship of IKB and CompatibL.

Registration is free, but places are limited. A PDF flyer is available with more information: download it here.

The proposed talks so far:

Peter Caspers, Negative rates in QuantLib / AD beyond typedef and operator overloading
Jörg Kienitz, A Short History of SABR — From approximation formulas to PDEs and back
Alexander Sokol, Implementing AAD in QuantLib
Eric Ehlers, Cloud computing in QuantLib
Klaus Spanderen, Calibration of Stochastic Local Volatility Models
Ferdinando M. Ametrano, Parametric basis rate curves
Sebastian Schlenkrich, Multi-curve Pricing of Non-Standard Tenor Vanilla Options in QuantLib
Andreas Pfadler, Proof of concept for a modern, distributed pricing architecture based on open source components
Roland Lichters, CSA Pricing using QuantLib
Luigi Ballabio, Ship it! QuantLib, Docker, and IPython Notebook
