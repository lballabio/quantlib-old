Mailing Lists

Choose a list to browse, search, and post messages.
  • quantlib-users:
    QuantLib users general discussion list. This list is for everything related to the QuantLib usage and development. Topics about quantitative finance in general are also accepted.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-announce:
    QuantLib announcement list (very low traffic). All major announcements about QuantLib will be posted here.
    You cannot post to this list. If you have an announcement for the QuantLib community, please post it on quantlib-users: the QuantLib admin will forward it to quantlib-announce if appropriate.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-jobs:
    Jobs list for supply and demand of quantitative finance jobs. You can post here: 1) job offers, 2) links to job offers, 3) your resume.
    Plain text, HTML, and PDF are preferred. Attachment are allowed as long as they are not too big. No multiple posts, please.
    All messages are archived for later browsing: please state how long your message is valid.
    (go to Archives, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)
  • quantlib-cvs:
    High-traffic list for automated QuantLib CVS checkin messages. Every time the QuantLib CVS code changes an email documenting the change is automatically generated.
    Suggested for developers only. Please do not directly post to this list.
    (not archived yet, Subscribe/Unsubscribe/Preferences)

Feedback and questions concerning this site and project can be directed to the QuantLib-users mailing list or the QuantLib administrator Ferdinando Ametrano (remove NOSPAM from the email address).
